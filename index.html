ABOUT

Joel Williams is a passionate educator and crop and soil health consultant who has primarily worked throughout Australia and the UK. He worked on conventional and organic farms improving sustainable farming practices in Australia before heading to the UK where Joel was investigating biological farming principles and providing soil chemical and biological assessments to primary producers throughout Europe. After this, Joel returned to Australia educating growers particularly on soil biology as well as working for Australia’s leading organic certification body. He is now based back in the UK where he is a full time grower at a London University creating an edible campus; growing organic vegetables to inspire and teach students, staff and local community about food growing – strengthening the connection between consumers and their food. He has a Bachelor of Agricultural Science specialising in crop and soil sciences and a keen interest in managing soil microbial balance and plant and soil nutrition to optimise plant immunity, soil health and carbon sequestration. He has a passion for teaching and sharing both scientific and practical knowledge on sustainable growing practices and has been fortunate enough to educate audiences in Australia, UK, Europe, South Africa, Canada and the US.
